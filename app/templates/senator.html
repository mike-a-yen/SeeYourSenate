About

Congress votes on about one new bill everyday. Keeping track of what your representative votes for and against is a daunting task, and it is even harder to determine if your senator is voting in the way you would vote.

Will It Pass keeps track of the voting history of each senator and highlights the important words in the bills voted for and against. This way you can get a quick summary of the voting behavior of your representative.
